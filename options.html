Notifications

Show Sample Notification

Push notifications can be shown on your desktop screen at a set time remaining from each class. For example, if notification time is set to 5 minutes, a notification will be sent when there is 5 minutes left in the period. Also, you can optionally select a 'ding' sound to play as well. By default, notifications are turned on. To turn off, click the slider in the top right. To see a sample notification, click the button in the top left.
